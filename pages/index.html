FAQ

Is there an airport pickup and drop-off service, and can I pick up and drop off the car at different locations?

Yes. Our car rental offers free airport pickup and drop-off services at major airports. There will be a one-way fee for drop-off locations other than the airport. We also offer flexible pickup and drop-off locations. Additional fees may apply depending on the distance.

Can I rent a car for a one-way trip outside of the pickup city?

No, our car rentals are only available for use within the designated area. The rental car must be returned to the designated drop-off location as per the rental agreement.

What should I do in case of an accident or breakdown?

Contact our emergency support team immediately. If necessary, inform the local authorities and your insurance provider. We will assist with roadside support and further instructions.

What happens if I return the car late?

A grace period of 45 minutes is allowed. After that, additional charges may apply, usually calculated based on hourly or full-day rates.

Are there any hidden fees or extra charges?

No, AmriTravel prices are all transparent. However, additional charges may apply for optional add-ons, late returns, extra mileage, or damages not covered by insurance.
